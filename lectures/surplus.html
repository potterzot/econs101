Price elasticity of demand

  • Price Elasticity of Demand - The change in quantity demanded that results from a change in price.

\[\varepsilon = \frac{\Delta Q / Q}{\Delta P / P}.\]

Two extremes of price elasticity of demand

  • Price elasticity of demand is zero: Change in quantity demanded for a (any) change in price is zero.

  • Price elasticity of demand is infinite: Change in quantity demanded for a (any) change in price is infinite.

Surplus

Consumer Surplus - The difference between what a buyer would have been willing to pay and the price they pay.

Producer Surplus - The difference between the price the good is sold for and the sellers cost to produce.
