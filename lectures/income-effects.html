Substitution effects

  • Substitution Effect - The effect (on quantity demanded) of a change in the price of a good as consumers substitute a good that has become relatively cheaper for the good that has become relatively more expensive.

Income effects

  • Income Effect - The effect (on quantity demanded) of a change in the price of a good due to the new prices effect on the consumers purchasing power.

Graphing Income and Substitution Effects

Giffen Goods

  • Giffen Good - A good that is an inferior good and that requires a substantial portion of the consumers income. As a result, for a price increasee, demand will actually increase, since the consumer becomes relatively poorer and so spends even more money on the inferior good.
